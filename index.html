  • About Us

    The Bay Area Breakers are a quidditch team based in the San Francisco Bay Area, established September 2018.

    Quidditch is a all-gender full-contact sport that was inspired by the magical sport in Harry Potter. It is a blend of rugby, basketball, and football.
